Modeling and design of tooling and fixtures

Designing fixtures for metal cutting machines
Not every metalworking project fits within standard parameters. At Metalwork India, we specialize in designing fixtures for metal cutting machines, developing tooling that enhances the precision of metal processing. One of our flagship projects was to make specialized tooling for the aviation industry, which led to 30% processing time reduction.
Learn More
Designing welding fixtures
Each welding fixture designed at Metalwork India is tailored to meet the unique production requirements of our clients, representing personalized welding solutions. As an example, we developed a welding fixture for a construction company that optimizes the welding process by ensuring perfect component alignment and improving the quality of weld seams.
Learn More
Designing assembly fixtures for mechanical assembly productions
Our assembly fixtures are notable for their ability to get easily integrated into any production process, ensuring precision in device assembly. Our solution for an electronic production— a jig for mechanical assembly—reduced assembly time by 40%, demonstrating our ability to adapt to specific client tasks.
Learn More
Designing transport fixtures (carts)
The uniqueness of our approach to designing transport fixtures is evidenced by the successful implementation of a project for a large warehouse complex. We developed a series of customized production carts optimized for the dimensions and weight of most frequently transported goods, which improved logistics efficiency at the facility by 35%.
