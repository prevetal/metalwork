What is an assembly jig?
The assembly jig from EA Metalwork Engineering India is an innovative metal structure in the form of a scissor lift. Its primary purpose is to provide comfortable conditions for workers, simplifying the execution of elevated assembly processes. The device allows for the positioning of personnel along with necessary tools and materials, ensuring safety and efficiency in the assembly process.
Key features of the jigs we design and manufacture:
Lifting mechanism
The jig's working platform is raised and lowered by a hydraulic cylinder, providing smooth and precise movement.
Power source
Â Standard models are equipped with a manual pump, but an integrated pneumatic-hydraulic pump can be added upon request to increase productivity.
Safety
The jig is equipped with a safety stop, railings on the working platform, a retractable ladder for access, and outriggers for stabilizing the structure.
Mobility
The jig has swivel wheels with brakes and handles for ease of movement, making it ideal for use in various industrial settings.
