Designing and manufacturing of cams

Order cams to enhance precision and speed up production.
Submit a Request

Cams play a crucial role in the securing and precision machining of workpieces on lathes and turning-milling machines. As integral parts of lathe chucks, they are highly functional. EA Metalwork Engineering India specializes in designing and manufacturing custom cams to meet the most exacting and specific production requirements. Our experience and professionalism allow us to create unique solutions that enhance efficiency and quality in machining processes.

What are custom cams?
Cams are essential components for securing workpieces in lathe chucks. They offer convenience and quick readjustment due to the simplicity of their replacement. Cams can be manufactured in both inch and metric sizes to meet customer requirements and specific manufacturing tasks. The materials used for creating cams are selected based on the needs and preferences of the client.
Types of cams
Solid cams
These are made from a single material and can be either soft or hardened.
Composite cams
Consist of several components, allowing for quick changes in their shape or size.
Materials for manufacturing cams
Steel
Used for high strength and durability.
Aluminum alloys
Used to reduce weight and machine load.

The design and manufacturing process of cams at EA Metalwork Engineering India
Meticulous design for maximum efficiency

Each project at EA Metalwork Engineering India begins with a thorough analysis of the individual client's requirements. This process allows us to precisely determine the necessary materials and machining technologies in order to create high-quality cams.

  • Requirement analysis. Our engineers thoroughly study technical and functional characteristics of the clients' production, which helps identify key design parameters.
  • Design development. Using advanced software, we develop detailed drawings that meet all standards and requirements.
  • Prototyping. Before starting mass production, we manufacture samples for testing and approval to ensure that each cam meets the specified characteristics.
  • Manufacturing. The manufacturing process involves the use of advanced technologies and high quality materials, ensuring the durability and reliability of each cam.
  • Quality control. Strict control at each stage of production ensures that each cam completely meets the precision and strength requirements.

Examples of cam applications
Examples of cam applications

Cams are widely used in both automatic and manual lathe chucks, where quick change of processed parts is required. They are ideal for batch production where every second counts. Due to their versatility, cams are an indispensable tool for machining parts in large volumes and ensure operational efficiency on the production floor.
