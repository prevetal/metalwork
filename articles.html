Modeling and design of tooling and fixtures
At «EA Metalwork Engineering», we recognize that the quality of any manufacturing process begins with accurate and thoughtful design. Our tooling and fixtures modeling and design services cover the full spectrum of your needs — from conceptual design to detailed technical modeling. Using the latest CAD and CAM programs, we create fixtures for metal cutting machines, welding, assembly, and transportation, tailored to the unique requirements of your production.

Fabrication of custom tooling and fixtures
Every detail in manufacturing matters, and at «EA Metalwork Engineering», this is a guiding principle when fabricating tooling and fixtures. Specializing in custom tooling, we offer a fully personalized approach, starting from a thorough analysis of your manufacturing challenges to producing high-quality tooling that precisely meets your requirements. Reverse engineering
Reverse engineering from «EA Metalwork Engineering» opens new opportunities for your business. This service allows not only to recreate and improve existing products but also to understand the deep technological principles of competing products, paving the way for innovations and enhancements. We offer a full range of reverse engineering services—from digital scanning to creating detailed 3D models and production drawings, giving you the key to deciphering and optimizing any engineering tasks.
