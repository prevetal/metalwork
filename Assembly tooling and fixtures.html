Assembly tooling and fixtures

At EA Metalwork Engineering India, we specialize in designing and manufacturing innovative assembly fixtures and jigs that help our clients enhance the efficiency and precision of their manufacturing processes. We offer solutions tailored to the specific needs of each product and production line.

Assembly fixture
We design and manufacture assembly fixtures to ensure efficiency and accuracy in assembly processes. These fixtures allow for precise holding of components in the required position, speeding up assembly and minimizing the likelihood of errors. We adapt each fixture to the specific requirements of your production, facilitating easy integration into any production line.
Learn More
Assembly jig
EA Metalwork Engineering India is an experienced developer and manufacturer of assembly jigs, which are ideal for handling large and heavy parts. Our jigs provide the necessary support and stability during the assembly process and can be enhanced with automation features, such as rotating mechanisms and automatic component feeding modules. As a result, the assembly process at your facility becomes much more efficient.
Learn More
Turnover device
We specialize in developing and manufacturing innovative turnover devices that greatly simplify the process of rotating and flipping large products during assembly, ensuring maximum accessibility to necessary parts. Our turnover devices are an integral part of assembling complex structures, as they allow for easy alteration of product orientation without the need to move them. This reduces the physical strain on workers and increases overall workplace safety.
