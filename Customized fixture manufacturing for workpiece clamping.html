Customized fixture manufacturing for workpiece clamping

Get a Consultation

EA Metalwork Engineering India provides professional services in manufacturing customized fixtures for clamping workpieces, considering the unique challenges of modern production. We focus on developing and creating bespoke solutions to enhance efficiency, precision, and processing quality in industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and machinery.

Understanding that standard solutions cannot always meet the specific needs of manufacturing processes, we offer our expertise and knowledge to develop fixtures that are perfectly suited to your business's tasks. Our clamping, machine, and setup fixtures are designed with every detail of the clamping process in mind, ensuring seamless and optimal production operations.

At EA Metalwork Engineering India, we strive to ensure our equipment performs its functions and significantly contributes to increasing overall productivity, reducing costs, and improving the quality of the final product. Relying on advanced technologies and personalized approaches, we guarantee that every fixture we manufacture will become a reliable link in your production chain.

Clamping fixture
Clamping fixtures are an integral part of milling and turning machines, and they are also widely used in processing long pipes and rods of various diameters. Our company specializes in manufacturing these fixtures, which significantly increase the precision and reduce the processing time of parts at automotive factories.
Example: To produce a new car model requiring a unique part shape, we developed a clamp that reduced setup time by 50% while ensuring repeatability with an accuracy of 0.01 mm.

Machine fixture
For a client in the aerospace industry, we created a machine fixture that processes titanium alloys for critical components. This fixture reduced error probability by 40% and speeded up production by 30%.

Setup fixture
